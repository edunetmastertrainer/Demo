About Me

The person behind the expertise

I am a Technocrat, Trainer, and Mentor with over 17 years of experience in providing technical education and shaping the careers of engineering students across India.

My journey spans academia, corporate training, and software architecture — from over 16 years as an Assistant Professor at ABES Engineering College to serving as Lead Technical Architect at DataRitz Technologies and now as Sr. Master Trainer at Edunet Foundation.

I specialise in bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and industry-ready skills, focusing on Python, Data Analytics, MERN Stack, and Full Stack Python development. My passion lies in problem-solving and cultivating the next generation of technical leaders.
